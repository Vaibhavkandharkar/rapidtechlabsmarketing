THE REAL PROBLEM

Why Most Local Businesses Lose Money on Meta Ads

Boosting a post is not performance marketing. Here's where 9 out of 10 ad accounts we audit are leaking budget before we ever touch them.

✕

No Pixel or CAPI Tracking

Without proper tracking, Meta has no idea who's actually converting — so it keeps showing ads to the wrong people.

✕

Audience Too Broad or Too Narrow

"Everyone in my city" isn't an audience. Generic targeting burns budget on people who'll never buy.

✕

Creatives People Scroll Past

Stock photos and plain text posts don't stop the thumb. Without a hook, the ad never gets seen.

✕

Zero Retargeting

97% of visitors don't convert on the first click. No retargeting means you're paying to forget them.

✕

Set & Forget Campaigns

Ad accounts left untouched for weeks fatigue fast — costs creep up while results quietly drop.

✕

"Likes" Mistaken for Leads

Vanity metrics look nice in a screenshot but don't pay your staff's salary. We report what actually matters.
